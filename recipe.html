Featured Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

Discover the classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal!

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Add egg mixture quickly to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese on top.

Tips:

  • Use fresh eggs for the best flavor.
  • Make sure to remove the pan from heat before adding the egg mixture to avoid scrambling.
  • Adjust the amount of cheese and pepper according to your taste.

Nutrition Information:

Calories: 600 per serving

  • Chicken Curry

    A flavorful chicken curry with spices and coconut milk.

    A comforting and flavorful curry with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce. Perfect with rice or naan!

    Preparation Time: 25 minutes

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Servings: 4

    Difficulty: Medium

    Ingredients:

    • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into cubes
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 large onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp grated ginger
    • 2 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
    • 1 cup chicken broth
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Heat oil in a large pot or pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from pot.
    2. Add onion to the pot and cook until soft and translucent.
    3. Stir in garlic, ginger, curry powder, and turmeric. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    4. Pour in chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
    5. Add the cooked chicken and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened.
    6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    Tips:

    • For extra flavor, you can marinate the chicken in a little yogurt and curry powder before cooking.
    • Adjust the spice level by adding a pinch of chili flakes or a finely chopped chili pepper with the garlic and ginger.
    • Serve with basmati rice or warm naan bread.

    Nutrition Information:

    Calories: 450 per serving
